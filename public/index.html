Welcome to Cinetropue,

Cinetroupe, is a one stop destination for you to be a film critic and also view what others think about a film. This was inspired by Letterboxd which is a very big and popular platform for film enthusiasts to log and write reviews about a film. Well, since I am a film enthusiast myself, I came up with this idea of sharing a platform similar to Letterboxd where you can write reviews about films. Be your own film critic.
